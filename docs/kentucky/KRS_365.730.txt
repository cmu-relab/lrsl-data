
365.730   Civil action for damages or injunction for violation of KRS 365.725 -- 

Rights and remedies. 

(1) Any customer injured by a violation of KRS 365.725 may institute a civil action to 

recover damages. 

(2) Any business that violates, proposes to violate, or has violated any provision of 

KRS 365.725 may be enjoined in a civil action. 

(3) The rights and remedies available under this section shall be cumulative to each 

other and to any other rights and remedies available under law. 

Effective: July 12, 2006 

History: Created 2006 Ky. Acts ch. 42, sec. 6, effective July 12, 2006.
