TITLE 11
Criminal Offenses

CHAPTER 11-49.3
Identity Theft Protection Act of 2015

SECTION 11-49.3-1


§ 11-49.3-1 Short title.

This chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Rhode Island Identity Theft Protection Act of 2015."

History of Section.
(P.L. 2015, ch. 138, § 2; P.L. 2015, ch. 148, § 2.)
